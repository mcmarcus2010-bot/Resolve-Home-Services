Resolve Home Services

Our Story

Built on a commitment to reliability, honesty, and quality — from a small local venture to Truro's trusted exterior cleaning company.

Resolve Home Services was built on a commitment to providing homeowners with reliable, high quality exterior cleaning and property maintenance services. Our focus is simple: deliver professional workmanship, clear communication, and results customers can trust.

Founded by Marcus McCully at the age of 12, the company began as a small local venture and has grown through hard work, attention to detail, and a dedication to customer satisfaction.

Today, Resolve Home Services continues to operate on the same principles it was founded on: reliability, honesty, and quality. We take pride in helping homeowners maintain and improve their properties while providing a professional and dependable service experience.
